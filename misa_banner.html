Management Inforamtion Systems Assoication Banner

Management Systems Information Student Association (MISA) is a student-organized club that promotes Management Systems Information (MIS) at Simon Fraser University. I created a series of branding materials for MISA. The branding materials include online posters, event photography, sponsorship package and promotional materials.

Aligning with extisting MISA Branding

One of the design constrain I had was to align the the banner with existing Club's branding. This including the limitation of using different colours and the choice of fonts. For this design, I decided to attend the club's events to better know its culture and as well as gather photographs that I could use for the design.

First Iteration

First, I created two iterations of the design to present to the club executive team memebrs to get their feedback.

Improvements

  • Absence of the colour white which is part of MISA Colour
